# Thèse ENS / CNRS (2008–2012)
Étude de la régulation du facteur d’élongation transcriptionnelle P-TEFb (HEXIM1 / Cyclin T1 / TAT).
Study of transcriptional regulation by P-TEFb complexes and their partners (HEXIM1, Cyclin T1, HIV-1 Tat).
Experimental work combining mutagenesis, protein interaction assays, microscopy, and molecular biology.
Provided functional insights into regulatory mechanisms of transcription elongation.
