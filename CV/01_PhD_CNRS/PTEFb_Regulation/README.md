# Regulation of P-TEFb by Cyclin T1, HEXIM1 and HIV-1 Tat

This repository consolidates the experimental work performed during my PhD
at IBENS (ENS Paris, 2008–2012) and the Retrovirology 2014 publication
focused on CyclinT1/HEXIM1/Tat interactions.

## Structure
- thesis/ — Manuscript, figures and supporting material
- retrovirology_2014/ — Experimental datasets for the 2014 publication
- methods/ — Consolidated experimental methods
- figures/ — Schematic figures (interaction maps, mutants, pathways)
- presentations/ — Posters and slides
